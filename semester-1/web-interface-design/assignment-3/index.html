GeForce RTX 40 Series GPU

The NVIDIA RTX 40 Series GPUs are high-performance graphics cards built on the latest Ada Lovelace architecture. These GPUs offer significant advancements in AI-driven features, real-time ray tracing, and enhanced performance for gaming, content creation, and AI workloads. They deliver impressive power efficiency, higher clock speeds, and expanded VRAM, making them ideal for demanding tasks like 4K gaming and complex 3D rendering.

  • Ada Lovelace Architecture: Latest GPU architecture for enhanced efficiency and performance.

  • DLSS 3: AI-powered performance boost using frame generation and deep learning for smoother gameplay.

  • 4th Gen Tensor Cores: Improved AI processing for faster workloads and superior upscaling.

  • 3rd Gen RT Cores: Advanced real-time ray tracing for more realistic lighting and reflections.

  • High VRAM Capacity: Up to 24GB of GDDR6X memory for handling heavy textures and data-intensive tasks.

  • PCIe Gen 5 Support: Faster data transfer for better performance in high-demand applications.

  • Energy Efficient: Lower power consumption despite increased computational power.
